About Us

Our Story

Learn about our church's history, values, and the community we've built together.

Our Journey of Faith

Founded in 1975, Restoration Endtime Church began as a small gathering of families committed to building a community centered on faith, love, and service. From humble beginnings in a local school auditorium, our congregation has grown into a vibrant community of believers dedicated to making a positive impact in our city and beyond.

Our church is guided by core values of inclusivity, compassion, and authentic faith. We believe in creating a space where everyone feels welcome, regardless of their background or where they are in their spiritual journey.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and love, inspiring spiritual growth and positive change in our community.

Our Mission

To create a loving community where people can encounter God, build meaningful relationships, and serve others.
